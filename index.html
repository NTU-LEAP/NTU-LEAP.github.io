Research

Our research group studies power electronics for electrification and advanced power conversion. We focus on the following research areas:

Power-Electronics-Dominated Power Systems

  • Hybrid AC/DC grid
  • Virtual inertia
  • Wide frequency oscillations
  • Grid-forming control
  • ...

High-Efficiency High-Power-Density Converters

  • DC-DC converter
  • T-Type inverter
  • GaN converter

Wireless Power Transfer

  • Advanced control and modulation
  • Precise dynamic modeling
  • Coil and magnetic core design
